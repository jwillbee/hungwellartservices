Installation & De-Installation

Installation

I can fully install your artwork in a gallery, home or office based on your specifications. Most hardware is provided with the exception of specialized installation materials. The price of installation is contingent on the estimated amount of hours to install. In the event that hand delivery is necessary (meaning I am going somewhere to pick up art or physically bringing art somewhere for you), travel is included in the estimate.
Price: 50.00 an hour. I can hang approximately 10 works in an hour depending on what they are.

Deinstallation

Deinstallation is significantly easier and can typically be achieved in under an hour.
Price: 50.00 per hour

Curation, Packaging

Curation

I am always very happy to offer my input and advice on how or where work should be hung. I’m also happy to help you make choices and lay out a show. I find those conversations very fun. It’s my favorite part. If I am asked to independently make the choices about where works are to be installed in a space I request a curation fee. The curation fee is only applicable to events and not private homes or businesses.
Price:The curation fee is 10.00 per each work hung. For example, if I curate 9 works, the curation fee is 90.00.

Packaging

Art works can be professionally packaged on site for storage or transport.
Price: 15.00 per artworks under 3’x3’
20.00 per artworks larger than 3’x3’

Tags

I curate many of my own shows and in this capacity have developed a system of google forms and docs for documenting the artists, the art works and the movement of the work from the artist to the gallery to the buyer or back to the artist. This is also a system I use to create tags for a show. This can be a time consuming and complicated task. If you are hosting a large group show and do not have a system of your own, I strongly recommend you consider this service. I do not recommend this option for exhibitions of three or less people.
Price: The price is considered based on the amount of artists and the amount of works in the exhibition. On average I ask for 10.00 per artwork and my hourly rate to develop the documents and track the work throughout the month.

Sales

Assistance with sales refers to assistance with the organizational aspect of sales, not the act of promoting the work or convincing buyers. It is a system of google forms and QR codes for collecting information from buyers, documenting payments, organizing delivery or providing receipts (or provenance) for the buyer. I use a system of posted QR codes for preferred payment methods and a google form to collect documentation that makes it easy for purchasers to buy work.
Price: Sales forms for individual shows can be created at a rate of 80.00 for the show. You can then monitor your own sales or sales can be monitored by me at a rate of 10.00 per artwork applicable only if the work sells.

Provenance

Provenance is an incredibly important and often overlooked element of an art show. Provenance provides a documented history of the artwork that dramatically impacts the value of the work over time. A fellow art handler once said to me “Art is like baseball cards for rich people.” Provenance is the stats on the back of the card. Individual provenance of each piece can be created and shared in a digital, printable format.
Price: 10.00 per artwork if photos of the work are provided or not desired. 15.00 per artwork to provide photos.

About Us

I am an artist, curator, teacher and art handler. I have 20 years of comprehensive experience facilitating the organization, curation and installation of art events. I have hung or installed many different types of art with values well exceeding seven figures. I have installed works in many different types of settings that include private homes, galleries, art fairs and businesses. I have worked for and with organizations like Christie’s Auction House in Rockefeller Center, The Green Project in New Orleans and Frank’s Chop Shop in Rochester. I have been with the Yards Art Collective since 2020. I offer the following services to assist in the various necessary steps to a successful art show. This is an ‘a la carte’ menu designed to help you receive exactly the services you need to have a great event. I can be as involved or uninvolved as you like in this process. Prices vary per service. If interested, an estimate is generated based on the amount of menu items and the size of the task. Reduced prices are considered when compiling multiple tasks. This service is designed to fit the individual needs of anyone looking for assistance with hanging artwork in a space or coordinating an event focused around the visual arts.
